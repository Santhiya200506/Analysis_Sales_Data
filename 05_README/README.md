📌 Project Overview



This project is an interactive Sales Data Analysis Dashboard developed using Microsoft Excel. It helps analyze retail sales data through Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, KPI Cards, and Slicers to provide meaningful business insights.



\---

🎯 Objectives



\- Clean and prepare the sales dataset.

\- Analyze sales performance.

\- Identify the highest-selling product category.

\- Identify the best-performing month.

\- Compare sales by gender.

\- Calculate the average customer age.

\- Build an interactive dashboard.



\---



🛠️ Tools Used



\- Microsoft Excel

\- Pivot Tables

\- Pivot Charts

\- Slicers

\- KPI Cards

\- Data Cleaning

\- Data Visualization



\---



📂 Dataset



The dataset contains the following fields:



\- Date

\- Gender

\- Age

\- Product Category

\- Quantity

\- Price Per Unit

\- Total Amount

\- Month

\- Day

\- Year



\---

📊 Dashboard Features



\- Total Sales KPI

\- Top Category KPI

\- Best Month KPI

\- Average Age KPI

\- Sales by Product Category Chart

\- Monthly Revenue Chart

\- Sales by Gender Chart

\- Top Products by Revenue Chart

\- Interactive Slicers



\---

💡 Key Insights



\- Electronics generated the highest sales.

\- May recorded the highest monthly revenue.

\- Female customers contributed more sales.

\- The average customer age is 41 years.
